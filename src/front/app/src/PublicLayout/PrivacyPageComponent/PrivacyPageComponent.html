Privacy Policy for AmethPong

Last updated: 20-11-2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal data when you use amethpong.fun, an online Pong game platform with user accounts, matches, tournaments and social features (the “Service”).

We are committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and applicable privacy laws.

1. Data Controller

AmethPong SL
Carrer d'Albert Einstein, 11, Nou Barris, 08042 Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain
Email: info@amethpong.fun

You can contact us at any time regarding how we handle your personal data.

We have not designated a Data Protection Officer (DPO) because our core activities do not meet the thresholds in Article 37 GDPR (we do not perform large-scale processing of special categories of data, nor regular and systematic monitoring of individuals, nor are we a public authority). If this assessment changes we will appoint a DPO and publish their contact details.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We only collect the data strictly necessary to operate the Service.

2.1. Data provided directly by the user

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored using secure hashing)
  • Birthdate (to verify that the user is 16+)
  • Avatar image uploaded voluntarily by the user

2.2. Data collected through Google Auth

If you register or log in using Google Auth, we receive only the data provided by Google necessary to authenticate you. We do not request or store your Google password.

2.3. Data needed for authentication

AccessToken (cookie)
RefreshToken (cookie)

These cookies are strictly necessary for login and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party cookies.

2.4. Technical connection data

IP address (transient use only)
We do not store IP addresses as logs; they are only used at the technical level to allow online connectivity between players.

We do not collect:

  • Analytics
  • Browsing logs
  • Advertising data
  • Geolocation
  • Third-party cookies
  • Behavioral or marketing data

3. Purposes of Processing

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  1. Account creation and authentication
  2. Allowing you to play matches and tournaments online
  3. Managing your profile, avatar and friends/block list
  4. Displaying your username and public profile to other users
  5. Providing verification emails and 2FA codes
  6. Ensuring the technical operation and security of the Service

We do not process data for marketing, analytics, advertising, or commercial profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Art. 6)

Purpose Legal Basis
Account creation and Service usage Contract performance (Art. 6.1.b)
User profile (username, avatar, etc.) Contract performance (Art. 6.1.b)
Google Auth login Consent (Art. 6.1.a)
Verification emails and 2FA Legitimate interest in ensuring security (Art. 6.1.f)
Age verification (16+) Legal obligation (Art. 6.1.c)
Technical operation and security Legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f)

5. Data Recipients

We only share data with service providers essential to run AmethPong:

  • Hosting provider
  • SMTP server for sending verification and 2FA emails
  • Google LLC when you use Google Auth to log in

We do not sell or share data with advertisers or third parties.

6. International Data Transfers

The only potential international transfer occurs when using Google Auth.

Google may process personal data outside the EU. These transfers are protected under:

  • The EU-US Data Privacy Framework (if applicable), or
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) adopted by the European Commission.

No other data transfers outside the EEA occur.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as your account remains active.

When you delete your account:

  • Personal data is deleted or anonymized (example: bob@gmail.com → deleted-user23134@gmail.com)
  • Files uploaded by you (e.g., avatar) are removed
  • Tokens become invalid
  • No persistent logs are kept

We do not store logs, analytics data, or historical tracking information.

8. Your Rights Under the GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to object
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent (for Google Auth login)
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority (AEPD in Spain)

To exercise your rights, contact us at: info@amethpong.fun

Users can also download all their data in JSON format through the platform.

9. Data Security

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure password hashing
  • HTTPS encryption
  • Token-based authentication
  • Restricted access controls
  • Validation of uploaded files (avatars)
  • Protections against unauthorized access and account misuse

10. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for login and session operation:

  • AccessToken
  • RefreshToken

These cookies:

  • Do not track you
  • Do not share data with third parties
  • Are exempt from cookie consent banners under the ePrivacy Directive (strictly necessary)

No analytics, marketing, or profiling cookies are used.

11. Age Requirement

The Service is intended for users aged 16 or older. We do not knowingly process the data of children under 16.

If we discover that a minor under 16 has registered, we will delete the account and its associated data.

12. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in our Service or legal requirements.

If the changes are significant, we will notify users through the website or via email.

The latest version will always be available at:
https://amethpong.fun/privacy
